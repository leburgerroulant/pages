Our Story

From humble beginnings to now

It's been our passion since the beginning of time to deliver the most unique tastes.

John Burger - Burger Connoisseur

Discovering Our Passion

We started as two brothers who shared a passion for food. We struggled to find a burger place that managed to meet our needs, so we did what any food lover does - we experimented in our kitchen to perfect the perfect recipes that we knew others would love!

It is that passion for discovering new food and recipes that keeps us going to ever improve upon ourselves. We hope that you love them just as much as we love creating them for you.


We strive to deliver you the perfect experience, every time

- The Burger Brothers

We're always looking for the next taste, and we have done since we were little.

Bob Burger - Head of Taste Research

Learning From Experience

Honing the perfect burger takes time. We made many mistakes along our journey in getting the recipe right though we knuckled down and stuck to it and this perseverance is used every day as we continue to increase the range of burgers we offer.


To this day, we are constantly evolving in the way we make our food and we impart this knowledge to our team of skilful chefs who help us further it even more. We are looking for those unique individuals who help us look at recipes from different angles.
